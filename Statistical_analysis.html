"""
Plotting the acf for log returns
Do not forget, acf plots are indicating internal structures, there is no need to keep any visible trend.
Thus, we use the log returns, and not the actual prices.
For this case (BTC), neither significant correlations found over the time nor any particular internal structure. 
"""
